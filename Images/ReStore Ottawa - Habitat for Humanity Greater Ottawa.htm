Habitat Greater Ottawa operates non-profit home improvement retail stores across Ottawa called Habitat ReStores, which sell donated, new and gently-used home furnishings, appliances and other renovation materials at a fraction of the original price.

The first Habitat ReStore in Canada was opened in Winnipeg, Manitoba in 1991 and the concept has since grown to include hundreds of locations across Canada and the U.S.

  • Seasonal products

All efforts are made to ensure consistency of pricing between the two ReStores operated by Habitat Greater Ottawa. Due to the unique nature of ReStore products, combination of new and gently used, and the frequently fluctuating stock, there may occasionally be price differentiation for similar items in our stores. Items are priced to sell at significantly below original retail value. Prices are as marked.
